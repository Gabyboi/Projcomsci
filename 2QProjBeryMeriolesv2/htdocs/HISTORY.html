Basketball was born on Springfield College which would
later be known as the famous International YMCA Training School.
Around the cold winter of 1892, the instructors had to find a
way create a substitute for outdoor games like football to
be played indoor while burning of the same almost amount
of energy. Naismith created one of the most phenomenal sports
ever, based off from many sports including American rugby,
English rugby, socker, lacrosse, and duck on a rock. From that
moment on the game of basketball and how it was played continues
to be changed by players. He observed how the students played
and changed rules for them to always keep the dribble alive,
have a clock shot, and the rest of it was history. After some
years, when the NBA (National Basketball Associtaion) was
founded the Hall of Famer Award was named after him.
